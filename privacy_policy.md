Privacy Policy — KeyBridge: BLE Keyboard & Mouse

Last updated: May 2, 2026

---

1. Overview

KeyBridge ("the App") is developed and maintained by Ivan (the "Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- The App does not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
- The App does not record, log, or transmit the text you type.
- The App does not access your contacts, camera, microphone, location, or files.
- The App does not use analytics, crash reporting services, or advertising SDKs.
- The App does not communicate with any remote server or cloud service.

3. Bluetooth Usage

The App uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) solely to communicate with a locally paired ESP32-S3 hardware device. All communication happens directly between your phone and the hardware device on your local network. No data is sent to the Developer or any third party.

4. Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

- BLUETOOTH_SCAN — to discover nearby BLE devices.
- BLUETOOTH_CONNECT — to connect to and communicate with the ESP32-S3 device.
- ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION — required by Android 11 and below for BLE scanning (no location data is collected or stored).

5. Data Storage

The App stores no user data on the device beyond basic operational state (such as the currently selected keyboard layout). This data never leaves your device.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs. It contains no ads.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same location with an updated date.
